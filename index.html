A Letter From My Heart

To My Love, Gabbie,

 

Happy first monthsary, my love.

 

It may just be one month on the calendar, but in my heart, it feels like so much more. Every day with you has been a blessing I'll never take for granted.

 

I know I can't buy you expensive gifts or be there with you today—and that really hurts me. If I could, I'd be by your side in a heartbeat. Kahit simpleng yakap lang, okay na ako, love. I may not have much, but everything I do have—lalo na puso ko—sa'yo lang talaga.

 

Sometimes I think back to the past—how I let you go before. And it still hurts, knowing that if things were different, we could've been celebrating a year or more by now. That thought never leaves me. But what matters now is we found our way back to each other. And this time, I promise I'm holding on for real.

 

I'm sorry if I fall short sometimes… if I make you mad, if I don't always show how much you mean to me. I know you deserve so much more, and I'm sorry if I haven't been enough. But please know, I'm trying. I'll always find ways to make up for my shortcomings—even through the smallest things—because you're worth every bit of it.

You are my peace, my happiness, my safe place.
I love you so deeply, Gabbie.

And while I can't be with you today, I'm hoping—if you're free—can I take you on a date tomorrow, Monday? Just us. No rush. Just love.

 

Here's to our first month… and to many more ahead.

Always yours,
Jaren
